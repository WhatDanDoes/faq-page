paraat.app

The Progressive Audio Recorder and Automatic Transcriber is a progressive web application that allows you to use your device's mic to record, send, and playback audio. It's currently wired to use Flashlight's English-trained software to perform an automatic transcription on every new audio file submitted.

All Saints Anglican Cochrane History

I used to volunteer at the Bethany Care Center in Cochrane. They had this neat little chapel which had been twice moved from its location over 125 years ago. I believe that this is the only digital copy of the church's history. I digitized it and wrote the Foreword.
