Hey Dan, what exactly do you do?

Great question! Thanks for asking...

wycliffe.photos

My progressive photo blogging app. It uses your device's camera without having to download a native app. I mostly take pictures of projects and stuff that made me laugh for some reason.

Take a Look!

paraat.app

The Progressive Audio Recorder and Automatic Transcriber is also a progressive web application. It allows you to use your device's mic to record, send, and playback audio. It's currently wired to use Flashlight's English-trained software to perform an automatic transcription on every new audio file submitted.

Have a Listen!

SIL Identity

Completed for SIL International. Identity provides instruction on how SIL's web products can best leverage the Auth0 authentication and authorization platform. Many of my apps are wired to use the Single-Sign-On social connections provided by Auth0 and the Single-Sign-Off implemented by the Identity app itself.

Try it Yourself

TOR New Living Translation

A privacy-oriented TOR-accessible Bible for believers in sensitive areas. You must download the TOR browser to access this app. With TOR, you do not reveal your location when accessing the web or darkweb.

This only works with TOR!

merman

The Ministry Expense Reiumbursement Manager is the tool I wrote to take pictures of receipts, catalog expenses, and bundle electronic files. I use to it manage ministry expenses for submission to Wycliffe Canada.

Catalog Your Ministry Expenses!

All Saints Anglican Cochrane History

I used to volunteer at the Bethany Care Center in Cochrane. They had this neat little chapel which had been twice moved from its location over 125 years ago. I believe that this is the only digital copy of the church's history. I digitized it and wrote the Foreword.

Learn Local History!
